The Los Angeles Lakers

About The Los Angeles Lakers

The Los Angeles Lakers is one of two teams that represent Los Angeles California in the National Basketball Association (Originally the Mineapolis Lakers) National Basketball Association.

There has been many storylines throughout the years such as Wilt dominating the 60s through Kobe and Lebron making sure the Lakers always competed.

Lakers Alltime Starting Five

  • Magic Johnson
  • Kobe Bryant
  • Lebron James
  • Kareem Abdul Jabbar
  • Shaquille O'Neal/Wilt Chamberlain

What Makes A Los Angeles Lakers Dynasty?

The Lakers have always prided themselves on having great leadership on and off the court. With Lebron opening up a school for low income students, to Shaq consistently helping out kids who related to his childhood. These values translated to being a leader on the court and made collaboration as a team easier.
